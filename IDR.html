About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is dedicated to advancing expertise in digital risk management. We bridge the gap between academia and industry by training skilled professionals who can identify, manage, and mitigate digital risks in modern organizations.

Our mission is to build a strong workforce capable of protecting businesses in an increasingly digital world.

Through collaboration with academic partners and industry leaders, IDR bridges the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world risk management practices.

Our Model

Academy

Training and bootcamps for students and professionals seeking careers in digital and cyber risk.

Innovation & Incubation

Developing future risk models, governance frameworks and AI risk research.

Advisory Services

Helping organisations implement security and governance frameworks.

Our Community

At IDR, we empower students, early-career professionals, and industry practitioners to build expertise in digital and cyber risk, enabling them to address real-world challenges across critical sectors.

Through industry-aligned training, research, and practical programs, IDR equips individuals with the skills needed to identify, manage, and mitigate emerging digital risks in today’s rapidly evolving technological landscape.
